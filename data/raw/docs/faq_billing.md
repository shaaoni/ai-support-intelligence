# Billing FAQ

## How do I update my payment method?
Log in to your account, go to Settings > Billing, and click "Update Payment Method". You can add a credit card, debit card, or PayPal account.

## Why was I charged twice?
Duplicate charges are rare but can occur during payment processing errors. Please contact support with your order ID and we will refund the duplicate charge within 3-5 business days.

## How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to Settings > Subscription > Cancel Plan. Your access continues until the end of the billing period. We do not offer partial refunds.

## Can I get an invoice?
Yes. Go to Settings > Billing > Download Invoice. Invoices are available for all transactions in the last 12 months.

## What happens if my payment fails?
We retry the payment after 3 days. If it fails again, your account is downgraded to the free tier. You will receive email notifications at each step.
