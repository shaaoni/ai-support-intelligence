# Product Wiki

## Plans and Pricing
We offer three plans: Free, Pro ($19/month), and Enterprise (custom pricing).
Free plan includes 5 projects and 1GB storage.
Pro plan includes unlimited projects and 50GB storage.
Enterprise includes SSO, audit logs, and dedicated support.

## Integrations
We support integrations with Slack, Jira, GitHub, and Zapier.
API access is available on Pro and Enterprise plans.
Full API documentation is at docs.example.com/api.

## Security
All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256 and TLS 1.3.
We are SOC2 Type II and GDPR compliant.
Two-factor authentication is available for all accounts.

## SLA and Uptime
We guarantee 99.9% uptime on Pro and Enterprise plans.
Scheduled maintenance occurs on Sundays between 2am and 4am UTC.
Status page is available at status.example.com.
