# Technical Support FAQ

## The app won't load. What should I do?
Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try again. If the issue persists, try a different browser or disable browser extensions.

## How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email and check your inbox for a reset link. The link expires after 30 minutes.

## Why is the app running slowly?
Slow performance is usually caused by a poor internet connection or high server load. Try refreshing the page or connecting to a different network.

## How do I export my data?
Go to Settings > Data > Export. You can export your data as CSV or JSON. Large exports may take up to 10 minutes.

## Is there a mobile app?
Yes. Download it from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). The mobile app supports all core features.
